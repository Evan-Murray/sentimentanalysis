Twitter Sentiment

Entering a term into the search bar will gather tweets containing the term you entered and analyze the polarity of tweets the term was found in. The results will be displayed in a generated graph once the analysis is finished. Each dot on the graph represents a tweet. The color of the dot indicates the overall sentiment: green = positive, red = negative, gray = neutral. The dot is placed vertically based on the level of polarity.
